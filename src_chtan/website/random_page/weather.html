Weather Forecasting

What is Weather Forecasting?

Weather forecasting is the application of science and technology to predict the conditions of the atmosphere for a given location and time. Forecasts are made by collecting data about the current state of the atmosphere and using meteorological models to predict future conditions.

Types of Weather Forecasts

  • Short-term Forecasts: Cover up to 3 days.
  • Medium-term Forecasts: Usually span 3 to 10 days.
  • Long-term Forecasts: Extend beyond 10 days and are generally less accurate.

Tools Used in Weather Forecasting

  • Satellites
  • Radar Systems
  • Weather Stations
  • Computer Models

Why is it Important?

Accurate weather forecasting helps in planning daily activities, protecting life and property, and aiding in sectors like agriculture, aviation, and shipping.
